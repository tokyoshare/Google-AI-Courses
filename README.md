## AI Using Scenario:
1. AI augmentation: This is the process where AI supports and enhances human capabilities. In this case, the AI handles the heavy lifting of drafting the description, and then the agent uses their experience and intuition to refine it. The AI doesn't replace the human; it helps them work faster and more creatively.

2. Replacement: If this were replacement, the AI would generate and publish the listing automatically without any human intervention or editing.

3. AI automation: This usually refers to repetitive processes performed entirely by machines from start to finish, with little to no creative human involvement in each step.

## How to use AI effectively:

To effectively and sustainably integrate AI into workflows, businesses should focus on human elements and cross-departmental coordination.

### We should:
1. **Maintain human oversight over the AI's output and decision-making processes**: AI can make mistakes or provide inaccurate information; human-in-the-loop oversight ensures the safety, ethics, and reliability of the final results.

2. **Promote cross-team collaboration to ensure the AI aligns with values that benefit people**: Implementing AI is not just a technical task; it requires input from various stakeholders (legal, HR, business) to ensure the tool serves its intended purpose without causing harm.

### We shouldn't:

1. **Only use the latest AI tools**: Using the absolute newest tool is less important than using the tool that is most suitable for the business's specific needs and budget.

2. **Rely entirely on computer engineers to keep AI solutions updated**: Relying solely on engineers creates a knowledge gap. For AI to be truly effective, the end-users (business staff) must also understand and participate in the operation and feedback loop.
